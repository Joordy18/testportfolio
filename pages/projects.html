JoordyBar

Context

JoordyBar is a project carried out during my free time with the sole aim of learning the HTML language.
The JoordyBar hotel wants to make its bar area known, and present its 5 house cocktails bearing the “refined” label.
The hotel therefore wants to allow its customers to discover its cocktails on the site in an original way that will highlight them, all while being accessible and easy to use.

Specifications

-Give crucial information about the bar
-Present the 5 cocktails offered by the bar

Respecting the following expectations:

-An easy-to-use, uncluttered interface
-Compliance with the 3 clicks rule
